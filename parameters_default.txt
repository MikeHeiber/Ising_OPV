## Parameters for Ising_OPV v2.0
-------------------------------
## General Parameters
50 //length (integer values only) (specify the x-direction size of the lattice)
50 //width (integer values only) (specify the y-direction size of the lattice)
50 //height (integer values only) (specify the z-direction size of the lattice)
true //enable_z_periodic_boundary (true or false) (periodic boundaries in x- and y-directions are enabled by default, but z-direction can also be enabled here)
0.5 //mix_fraction (donor fraction)
0.3 //interaction_energy1 (code optimized for values from 0.3 to 1.0) (energetic favorability for type1-type1 interactions over type1-type2 interactions in units of kT)
0.3 //interaction_energy2 (code optimized for values from 0.3 to 1.0) (energetic favorability for type2-type2 interactions over type1-type2 interactions in units of kT)
200 //MC_steps (determines the extent of phase separation)
-------------------------------
## Export Morphology Options
true //enable_export_compressed_files (true or false) (chose whether or not morphology file output is in compressed format or not)
false //enable_export_cross_section (true of false) (chose whether or not to separately output uncompressed data for the cross-section at the x=0 plane)
-------------------------------
## Smoothing Options
true //enable_smoothing (true or false) (chose whether or not to execute the domain smoothing process)
0.52 //smoothing_threshold (0.52 is optimal value determined when domain size > 5 nm)
-------------------------------
## Rescale Morphology Options
false //enable_rescale (true or false) (chose whether or not to rescale the lattice after phase separation is complete, smoothing is performed after rescaling if both are enabled)
2 //rescale_factor (integer values only)
-------------------------------
## Interfacial Mixing Options
false //enable_interfacial_mixing (true or false) (chose whether or not to introduce interfacial mixing after phase separation, smoothing, and rescaling is complete)
4 //interface_width (nm) (specify the approximate width of the interfacial mixing region)
0.5 //interface_conc (donor fraction) (specify the mixing concentration in the interfacial region)
-------------------------------
## Analysis
false //enable_analysis_only (true or false)
true //enable_correlation_calc (true or false) (chose whether or not to calculate the domain size using the pair-pair correlation method)
100000 // N_sampling_max (specify the maximum number of sites to randomly sample for the correlation calculation)
true //enable_interfacial_distance_calc (true of false) (chose whether or not to calculate the interfacial distance histograms)
true //enable_tortuosity_calc (true or false) (chose whether or not to determine the tortuosity histograms, calculate the island volume fraction, and collect the end-to-end path data)
